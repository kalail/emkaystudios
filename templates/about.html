    About

    Founded by Emad Khan and Kashif Malik, EmKay Studios specializes in 3-D design, Video production and Sound composition.

    3-D design encompasses stand alone projects as in the case of 3-D modeling for architecture, products and visual spaces. It also includes integration of 3-D elements into live video footage for example movie special effects.

    Video production includes all three stages: Pre-production i.e. script writing and idea generation, Production i.e. directing and producing on site shooting and Post-production i.e. editing, video design, color grading, special effects and more.

    Sound composition compliments video production where original scores are to be composed for projects. Music can be composed separately where required. We also provide Sound mastering and Editing services.
